## 5 Nights 5 Days Uzbekistan Tour Package  
*(Date: 01/12/24 – 06/12/24)*  

### Day 1: Arrival in Tashkent  
- **Arrive at Tashkent International Airport.**  
- **Transfer to hotel and check-in.**  
- **Visit Khast Imam Complex, Chorsu Bazaar, and Old City.**  
- **Explore Independence Square, Amir Timur Square, and the Museum of Applied Arts.**  
- **Overnight stay in Tashkent.**  

### Day 2: Tashkent to Samarkand  
- **Take the high-speed train to Samarkand (approx. 2 hours).**  
- **Visit Registan, Gur-e-Amir Mausoleum, and Bibi-Khanym Mosque.**  
- **Explore Shah-i-Zinda Necropolis and Siyob Bazaar.**  
- **Overnight stay in Samarkand.**  

### Day 3: Samarkand to Bukhara  
- **Take the high-speed train to Bukhara.**  
- **Visit Lyab-i-Hauz Ensemble, Kalyan Minaret, and Ark of Bukhara.**  
- **Explore Po-i-Kalyan Mosque, Trading Domes, and Old City.**  
- **Overnight stay in Bukhara.**  

### Day 4: Bukhara to Tashkent  
- **Full day dedicated to exploring Bukhara’s rich historical sites.**  
- **Visit Ark Fortress, Kalon Minaret, Chor Minor Madrasah, Bolo Hauz Mosque, Mir-i-Arab Madrasa, and Ulugʻbek Madrasa.**  
- **Evening train at 4:41 PM to Tashkent.**  
- **Overnight stay in Tashkent.**  

### Day 5: Tashkent - Amirsoy & Departure  
- **Drive to Amirsoy Mountain Resort (approx. 2 hours).**  
- **Optional hike to the top of Amirsoy Mountain.**  
- **Visit Chimgan Mountains for scenic views.**  
- **Enjoy activities at Charvak Lake (boat rides, swimming, relaxation).**  
- **Return to Tashkent and transfer to the airport at 10 PM.**
