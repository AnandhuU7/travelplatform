## 5 Nights 5 Days Uzbekistan Tour Package  
*(Date: 01/12/24 – 06/12/24)*  

### Day 1: Welcome to Uzbekistan  
- **Arrive at Tashkent International Airport**  
- **Airport pickup and transfer to hotel**  
- **Visit:**  
  - Khast Imam Complex  
  - Chorsu Bazaar and Old City  
  - Independence Square  
  - Amir Timur Square  
  - Museum of Applied Arts  
- **Overnight stay in Tashkent**  

### Day 2: Tashkent to Samarkand  
- **Travel to Samarkand via high-speed train (approx. 2 hours)**  
- **Visit:**  
  - Registan Square  
  - Gur-e-Amir Mausoleum  
  - Bibi-Khanym Mosque  
  - Shah-i-Zinda Necropolis  
  - Siyob Bazaar  
- **Overnight stay in Samarkand**  

### Day 3: Samarkand to Bukhara  
- **Travel to Bukhara via high-speed train**  
- **Explore:**  
  - Lyab-i-Hauz Ensemble  
  - Kalyan Minaret  
  - Ark of Bukhara  
  - Po-i-Kalyan Mosque  
  - Trading Domes  
  - Old City exploration  
- **Overnight stay in Bukhara**  

### Day 4: Bukhara to Tashkent  
- **Full-day exploration of Bukhara's historic sites:**  
  - Ark Fortress  
  - Kalon Minaret  
  - Chor Minor Madrasah  
  - Bolo Hauz Mosque  
  - Mir-i-Arab Madrasa  
  - Ulugʻbek Madrasa  
  - Mazar of Sheikh Bahaudhen Naqshabandh (R)  
- **Evening train at 4:41 PM to Tashkent**  
- **Overnight stay in Tashkent**  

### Day 5: Tashkent – Amirsoy & Departure  
- **Drive to Amirsoy Mountain Resort (approx. 2 hours)**  
- **Explore:**  
  - Hike to Amirsoy Mountain (optional)  
  - Visit Chimgan Mountains  
  - Relax at Charvak Lake (boat ride, swimming, or leisure time)  
- **Return to Tashkent and airport drop-off at 10:00 PM**  
- **End of trip with unforgettable memories**
