### Day 1: Tashkent - Amirsoy  
- **Drive to Chinorkent (approx. 2 hours).**  
- **Hike to Amirsoy Mountain (optional, ticket not included).**  
- **Visit Chimgan Mountains and Charvak Lake for scenic views and activities such as cable car, horse riding, buggy bike ride, paragliding, skiing, and snow activities.**

### Day 2: Tashkent to Samarkand  
- **Travel to Samarkand by train.**  
- **Hotel check-in and leisure time.**  
- **Samarkand city tour: Shah-i-Zinda, Registan Square, Bibi-Khanym Mosque.**  
- **Experience the architectural wonders and cultural heritage of Uzbekistan.**  
- **Overnight stay in Samarkand.**  

### Day 3: Samarkand to Tashkent  
- **Breakfast at the hotel.**  
- **Continue Samarkand city tour: Konigil Village, Wine Factory.**  
- **Return to Tashkent in the evening by train.**  

### Day 4: Tashkent City Tour & Departure  
- **Hotel pickup after breakfast.**  
- **Visit Khast Imam Complex, Chorsu Bazaar, and Old City.**  
- **Explore Independence Square, Amir Timur Square, and the Museum of Applied Arts.**  
- **Transfer to Tashkent International Airport for departure.**
