## 3 Nights 4 Days Uzbekistan Tour Package  
*(Date: 14/11/24 – 18/11/24)*  

### Day 1: Arrival in Tashkent  
- **Arrive at Tashkent International Airport (10:00 PM arrival)**  
- **Welcomed by our Hindi/English-speaking travel guide**  
- **Head to the destination for an overnight stay (no hotel included)**  

### Day 2: Tashkent – Amirsoy & Chimgan Mountains  
- **Drive to Chinorkent (approx. 2 hours)**  
- **Explore Amirsoy Mountain (optional hike, ticket not included)**  
- **Visit Chimgan Mountains for scenic views**  
- **Activities:**  
  - Cable car  
  - Horse ride  
  - Buggy bike ride  
  - Paragliding  
  - Skiing & snow activities  
- **Return to Tashkent**  
- **Magic City tour and overnight stay at the hotel**  

### Day 3: Tashkent to Samarkand  
- **Travel to Samarkand via train**  
- **Hotel check-in and leisure time**  
- **Samarkand city tour:**  
  - Shah-i-Zinda  
  - Registan Square  
  - Bibi-Khanym Mosque  
- **Explore the architectural wonders of Uzbekistan**  
- **Evening walking tour in the Eternal City**  
- **Overnight stay in Samarkand**  

### Day 4: Samarkand to Tashkent & Departure  
- **Morning Samarkand city tour:**  
  - Konigil Village  
  - Wine Factory  
- **Return to Tashkent via train in the evening**  
- **Final city tour:**  
  - Khast Imam Complex  
  - Chorsu Bazaar & Old City  
  - Independence Square  
  - Amir Timur Square  
  - Museum of Applied Arts  
- **Transfer to Tashkent International Airport for departure**  
- **End of the trip with unforgettable memories**
