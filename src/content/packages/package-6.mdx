## 6 Nights 7 Days Turkey Tour Package  
*(Date: 31/03/25 – 06/04/25)*  

### Day 1: Arrival in Istanbul  
- **Arrive at Istanbul International Airport (6:00 PM arrival)**  
- **Private transfer to hotel and check-in**  
- **Free evening for leisure or dinner**  
- **Overnight stay in Istanbul**  

### Day 2: Istanbul City Tour  
- **Full-day Istanbul exploration:**  
  - Hippodrome Square  
  - Blue Mosque  
  - Hagia Sophia Museum & Grand Mosque  
  - Topkapi Palace (Entry included)  
  - Grand Bazaar  
  - Galata Tower (Photoshoot)  
  - Evening Bosphorus dinner cruise (unlimited soft drinks, cultural performances)  
- **Overnight stay in Istanbul**  

### Day 3: Flight to Cappadocia & Exploration  
- **Morning flight to Kayseri/Nevşehir (Cappadocia)**  
- **Visit:**  
  - Devrent Valley (Imagination Valley)  
  - Pasabagi (Monk’s Valley)  
  - Avanos (Pottery center)  
  - Göreme Open Air Museum  
  - Uchisar Castle & Underground City  
- **Overnight stay in Cappadocia**  

### Day 4: Cappadocia to Antalya via Konya  
- **Optional early morning Hot Air Balloon tour**  
- **Breakfast & Transfer from Cappadocia to Antalya via Konya**  
- **Visit Mevlana Mausoleum**  
- **Overnight stay in Antalya**  

### Day 5: Antalya City & Waterfall Tour  
- **Full-day Antalya sightseeing:**  
  - Karpuzkaldıran Waterfall  
  - Duden Waterfall  
  - Stroll through Konyaaltı Beach  
  - Kaleiçi (Antalya’s Old Town)  
  - Panoramic Boat Tour  
- **Overnight stay in Antalya**  

### Day 6: Antalya to Pamukkale  
- **Transfer to Pamukkale for a full-day tour:**  
  - Hierapolis Ancient City  
  - Necropolis  
  - Roman Baths  
  - Plutonium & Theatre  
  - Antique Pool & White Terraces  
- **Overnight stay in Pamukkale**  

### Day 7: Departure from Denizli Airport  
- **Morning breakfast and check-out**  
- **Transfer to Denizli Airport for return flight to Istanbul**  
- **Departure from Turkey**  
- **End of trip with unforgettable memories**  

## Total Package Price: 4300 USD (for 3 pax)
